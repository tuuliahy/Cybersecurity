Privacy Policy

At Booking System, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our booking system and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with the best experience when using our service:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, password, and other details provided during registration or profile creation.
  • Booking Information: Details about your bookings, such as dates, resources reserved, and any preferences you specify.
  • Payment Information: If applicable, your payment details for completing a booking (such as credit card information) will be handled securely through a third-party payment processor.
  • Cookies and Usage Data: We use cookies to improve user experience, track session data, and gather analytics about how you interact with our website.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate account registration, login, and personalization of your experience.
  • To manage your bookings, including the creation, modification, and cancellation of resource reservations.
  • To communicate with you regarding your bookings, service updates, promotions, and important notifications.
  • To analyze usage trends, improve our services, and ensure the security of our system.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share information with trusted service providers, such as payment processors, for the purpose of completing transactions or improving our services. We will only share your data when required by law.

Data Security

Your personal data is protected with appropriate security measures, including encryption, to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.
